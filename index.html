Our services

Comprehensive Health home care services.

Medical care consultancy

We focus on providing quality, accessible healthcare for the whole family. The majority of our medical providers are family practice, that means when you come to Community Health Care you can take care of all of your children’s health needs, as well as to the whole community.

Laboratory service test

We offer clinical labratory service test provided by a medical lab that aid in diagnosis and treatment of patients. The services include; Blood count, Urinalysis, Cholestrol test, tansfusion medicine, haemoglobin test, therapeutic drug monitering, diagnosis testing, etc

physiotherapy and occupational Therapy

SHF have qualified physiotherapist and occupational therapist that focus on treating a client's injury and working to improve his/her ability to perform movement of the human body and perform activities of daily living independently following a period of physical impairment

Expert nutrition care

Stable Health Foundation have nutritionist experts,to promote health and manage diseases. These specialists advise people on what to eat in order to lead a healthy lifestyle or achieve a specific health-related goal. We achieve this through sequential Medical camps across the nation.

psycho Social Care

SHF partners with community social workers to promote physical and mental wellness and their ability to function. People may not be fully aware of the relationship between their mental and emotional wellbeing and the environment, Psychosocial care must continue to address the needs of the family as death imminently approaches and after death occurs.

Chronic Diseases

A chronic condition is a human health condition or disease that is persistent or otherwise long-lasting in its effects or a disease that comes with time. Chronic diseases - such as heart disease, cancer, diabetes, stroke, and arthritis - are the leading causes of disability and death in our communities. SHF puts effort to address on how to live and manage these diseases.

Dental services

Stable Health Foundation is Equiped with professional dentist to provide treatment on Gum disease,toothache, Stained Teeth, Cavities, Chipped Tooth, Impacted Teeth, Cracked Tooth cavities, oral cancer, dry mouth, and bacteria on the tongue are some of the dental problems that can cause bad breath.

Optical services

Eye & Vision problems have been a major challenge in our communities. Stable Health foundation has mobilized qualified Opticians and has opened clinics in local communities to address this challenge. Eye diseases can cause damage and blindness if not treated soon enough. contact us to lias with our Medical camps schedule to find the best eye & vision treament.

Pharmacy services

Our professional pharmacy services help save you time and money. Our pharmacists help you manage ongoing health conditions and keep you informed on everything from new vaccinations to health insurance changes. SHF Pharmacist is always available to help you find the services that are right for you. We provide free local delivery around our clinics.
